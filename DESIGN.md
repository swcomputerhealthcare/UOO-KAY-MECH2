# Design System: UOO KAY MECH INDUSTRIES

A visual, structural, and motion guide for engineering serious, expensive B2B manufacturing interfaces. Inspired by the clean catalog layouts of German engineering firms like Siemens, Bosch, ABB, and Festo.

---

## 1. Visual Theme & Atmosphere
The design system enforces a highly structured, sober layout with generous whitespace, solid boundaries, and technical clarity. It avoids flashy SaaS gradients, rounded glass buttons, and bento grid layouts. It looks like a catalog built by a company that has manufactured precision parts for 25 years.

- **Serious & Industrial:** The vibe is confidence and restraint.
- **Expensive & Technical:** Solid structure, thin solid dividers, and precise tabular listings.
- **Anti-SaaS:** Absolutely no neon highlights, glowing shadows, floating cards, or rounded pill buttons.

---

## 2. Color Palette & Roles
A unified, restricted palette of warm stone and burnt copper. Blue and cyan accents are completely banned.

- **Background** (`#F6F5F3`) — Warm stone/paper grey canvas.
- **Text** (`#151515`) — Deep off-black.
- **Secondary** (`#666666`) — Muted grey for descriptions, subheads, and secondary metadata.
- **Border** (`#D9D9D9`) — Solid light-medium grey for structures and dividing lines.
- **Accent** (`#C46A2D`) — Burnt copper/orange accent for focused action items and highlights.

---

## 3. Typography Rules
Typography is structured for technical legibility and catalog-like alignment.

- **Display/Headlines:** `Barlow Condensed` (Google Fonts) — Upper-case, track-tight, and bold/medium weights. Used for product headers and categories.
- **Body & Paragraphs:** `IBM Plex Sans` (Google Fonts) — Serious, highly readable geometric sans-serif.
- **Specs & Data:** `IBM Plex Mono` (Google Fonts) — For dimensions, capacities, standards, and certificates.
- **Banned:** `Inter` and display serifs are banned.

---

## 4. Layout Principles
- **No Bento Grids:** Bento grids are banned as default template slop.
- **No Card Wrappers:** Most sections should not use cards. Rely on whitespace, grid alignments, and horizontal dividing lines (`border-t border-[#D9D9D9]`) instead.
- **Editorial Alternation:** Page content should alternate in a clean, vertical magazine style:
  1. Large Image / Small Text Block
  2. Large Text Block / Small Image
  3. Repeat
- **Responsive Stack:** All columns collapse to a single-column layout on viewports below `768px` with consistent `px-4`.

---

## 5. Animations
All animations must be minimal and unnoticeable.
- **Allowed:** Fade, Slide, Scale on scroll triggers.
- **Banned:** Magnetic buttons, floating cards, blob gradients, mouse followers, particle systems, and 3D backgrounds.

---

## 6. Photography Rules
Only real, authentic industrial visual assets:
- Actual machine tools, CNC centers, lathes, and workshop floors.
- Close-ups of raw metals, sheet fabrications, welding sparks, and CAD blueprints.
- **Banned:** AI-generated factory slop, stock models smiling in clean helmets, and office environments.
